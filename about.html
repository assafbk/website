Hi! I'm Assaf

I am a PhD candidate at Tel Aviv University, advised by Prof. Raja Giryes. My research focuses on improving Large Language Models, with a broader goal of investigating General Artificial Intelligence.

Previously, I worked on a few Deep RL projects at CRML Lab (Technion), researched methods for Audio Enhancement with Array Signal Processing, and worked in the Autonomous Vehicle industry, developing signal processing algorithms for mm-wave RADARs. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the Technion, Israel (Magna Cum Laude).
